About the Author

Rodrigo Madriz is a Toronto-based technology entrepreneur, educator, and product strategist. He has held senior roles at TELUS, Bell Canada, and MLSE, completed two startup exits, and currently builds and operates multiple live digital products — including HomeGadgets.ca, BasketBrain.ca, and ContextQuant — as a solo founder using Claude as his primary development partner.

He holds adjunct faculty status at Toronto Metropolitan University's graduate engineering program.
